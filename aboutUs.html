LFC Technologies - About Us

LFC Technologies is a family-owned company based in Boston, MA, ( 'The Town' ). Focused on providing personalized customer services at competitive market rates, this company serves the IT needs of small-to-mid-sized businesses and residential clients across the Metro-Boston area. We offer a selection of services: Managed IT Services, Data Backup and Disaster Recovery, On-demand Troubleshooting, Hardware and Software Setup and Configuration, Server and Network Design, Technology Consulting and Help Desk Support. LFC Technologies has the certifications, experience and partnerships to manage your company’s IT infrastructure. Our cost-effective service plans will allow your business to focus on what it does best with minimal downtime.
